1981

IEEE USA career maintenance and development task force sponsors a national conference on careers

Empowering Women

1994

The IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) program is introduced. It's mission: to increase the percentage of female engineers and generate greater awareness of their contributions.

2001

IEEE launches it's Job Site. Members post resumes. Employers list job openings.

e-learning

2011

The eLearning Library is unveiled to house IEEE’s collection of continuing-education courses.

2014

IEEE WIE holds it's first leadership conference, in San Francisco.

Networking

2015

IEEE Collabratec gets underway. The online platform offers a suit of tools for networking, collaborating, innovating and mentoring.

2016

IEEE introduces it's entrepreneurship community, which offers online and in-person networking events.

leadership

2017

The IEEE TechEthics website launches, It contains information on the ethical and societal implications of technology in support of IEEE members’ roles as thought leaders and trusted resources.
